We have named our token Mad.AI because we believe that the power of artificial intelligence can sometimes seem like magic. The incredible capabilities and potential of AI can leave us in awe, and we wanted a name that reflected that sense of wonder and excitement.


Additionally, the name Mad.AI also implies a sense of innovation and boldness, which aligns with our mission to push the boundaries of what is possible with AI technology.


Our vision & utility

Our vision is to create a decentralized AI-powered platform for mental health diagnosis and treatment. The platform could use the Mad.AI token as a means of payment for accessing various services such as online counseling, therapy sessions, and medication prescriptions. The platform could also incentivize users to contribute their data to improve the accuracy of the AI algorithms, and they could earn Mad.AI tokens as a reward for doing so. This would create a self-sustaining ecosystem where users can receive mental health treatment while contributing to the improvement of the platform.
